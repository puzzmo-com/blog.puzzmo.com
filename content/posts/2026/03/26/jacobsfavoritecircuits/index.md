After writing *almost a thousand puzzles* over the last few years, Circuits creator [Jacob Land](https://www.puzzmo.com/user/cir/jland) has put together a list of some of his favorites. (The Circuits on this list are now free to play\!)

**Jacob:** I’ve designed about 800 Circuits puzzles. A few of them are quite good. Even with Puzzmo’s fancy internal database, there’s not really a way to search for “my best puzzles.” So as I sought to make a list of my all-time favorites, I relied mostly on what stuck out in my memory—usually puzzles with an unexpected theme or a lively couple of phrases. I hope you enjoy these.
